The CNFP (Computer Science, Music, Robotics, Self-improvement, Sustainable Development, Languages, Books, Social Sciences, Audiovisual, Communication, Sports, Games, and Culture) is a distinguished scientific and social club catering to university students who share a profound enthusiasm for various fields including computer science, music, robotics, self-improvement, sustainable development, languages, literature, social sciences, audiovisual arts, communication, sports, games, and culture. Additionally, it serves as an essential support network for those seeking assistance. Founded in October 2019, the CNFP Club emerged from the initiative of ambitious young scholars from the Faculty of Exact and Applied Sciences at the University of Oran 1 Ahmed Ben Bella. Their interests spanned computer science, chemistry, and robotics. Since 2017, the CNFP Club has been actively collaborating with the Agence Universitaire de la Francophonie in the development of scientific engagement opportunities for young university students. It's important to note that the CNFP operates entirely through the dedicated efforts of its members, who serve on a voluntary basis. This exclusive body is comprised solely of active members selected during the general assembly. Upon completion of their university studies, members have the option to join the esteemed alumni community.
